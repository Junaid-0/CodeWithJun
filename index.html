ROING

The snow peaked mountains, deep gorges, turbulent rivers cascading down the hills, numerous waterfalls, placid lakes, archaeological sites, peace and fun loving hospitable people and above all the heavenly climate makes Roing one of the most attractive destinations for tourists in Arunachal Pradesh.
